# NNNN — One-line title in the present tense

- **Status:** Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by NNNN
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** name(s)

## Context

What's the situation? What forces are at play (technical, product, team)? What problem are we trying to solve, and why now?

Keep it to a couple of paragraphs. Reviewers should be able to read the context and predict roughly which way you're going to land.

## Decision

What we chose to do, in one paragraph. Lead with the action ("Build on X", "Use Y", "Defer Z until …"), not the discussion.

## Consequences

What gets easier, what gets harder, what we're now committed to.

- **Easier:** …
- **Harder:** …
- **Committed to:** …

## Alternatives considered

What else was on the table and why we passed. One bullet each, with a sentence on the reason.

- **Option B.** Why we didn't pick it.
- **Option C.** Why we didn't pick it.
